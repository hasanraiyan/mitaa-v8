The MITAA Advantage

The MIT Alumni Association (MITAA) is the official body dedicated to strengthening the bonds between Muzaffarpur Institute of Technology and its diverse global alumni network.

We provide a robust platform for professional networking, continuous learning, mentorship, and celebrating the remarkable achievements of our graduates, while actively supporting the strategic goals of the Institute.
